The office of the Web Administrator will setup accounts for the faculty members, officers, admin staff and post graduate students of University of Sindh Jamshoro and its affiliated campuses. For availing this facility users need to fill out the relevant data collection form in this regard.

After downloading the form please complete the blank fields and return this form to the Office of the Web Administrator and don’t forget to mention your existing email address and cell phone number as we will be creating an official email account for you on the domain of University of Sindh.
