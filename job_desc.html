Teaching Assistant Required at the Institute of Gender Studies, University of Sindh, Jamshoro

Applications are invited for the Temporary/contract One (01) post of Teaching Assistant in the Institute of Gender Studies, University of Sindh, Jamshoro.

The applicants should possess First Class or 3.0 CGPA in BS (4 years) / Master’s degree / M.Phil degree in Gender Studies from any HEC recognized University and having no 3rd division / D grade in the academic career.

The candidates are required to submit their application on the prescribed application form (editable) given below with a coloured photograph (pasted on the form) in the office of the undersigned latest by September 4th, 2024 by 3:00 pm along with attested copies of the following documents.

 

  1. Certificates/testimonials
  2. Passport-sized photographs (02 copies)
  3. CNIC
  4. Domicile/PRC


Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for written test/interviews. The University of Sindh reserves the right to cancel some or all the vacancies.

 

Prof. Dr. Misbah Bibi Qureshi
Director
Institute of Gender Studies
University of Sindh, Jamshoro
