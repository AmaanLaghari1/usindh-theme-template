Teaching Assistants Required in Department of Library & Information Science & Archive Studies, University of Sindh, Jamshoro

Applications are invited for contractual posts of Teaching Assistant (3) in the Department of Library & Information Science & Archive Studies, University of Sindh, Jamshoro.

The applicants should possess First Class or 3.00 CGPA in BS (4 years) degree in the relevant field, having Sixteen (16) years of education from any HEC recognized University and having no 3rd division / D grade in academic career.

The candidates are required to submit their application on the prescribed application form (editable) given below with a colored photograph (pasted on the form) in the office of the undersigned latest by 07-03-2023 along with attested copies of the following documents.

  1. Certificates/testimonials
  2. Passport sized photographs (02 copies)
  3. CNIC
